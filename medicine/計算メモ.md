## メモ

### 輸液

#### GIR計算

- GIR = WQ x Dx x 0.694
- WQ = GIR ÷ Dx ÷ 0.694
- Dx = GIR ÷ WQ ÷ 0.694
- 10% Tz : GIR = WQ x 0.0694
- S3AG: GIR = WQ x 0.0521
- S3A: GIR = WQ x 0.0298
